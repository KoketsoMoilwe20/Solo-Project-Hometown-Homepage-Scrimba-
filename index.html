Visit Rustenburg

The most populous city in the North West province, South Africa.
Enjoy the key attractions in Rustenburg.

To three activities to do in Rustenburg:

Sun City Casino

Experience the legendary glitz and glamour of Sun City Casino. Opened in 1979, the casino has evolved with the times and the latest technology, popular games combine a spectacle of round-the-clock thrills for both experienced and first-time gamers.

Kgaswane Nature Reserve

The Kgaswane Mountain Reserve set in the hills above the town of Rustenburg, spans some forty-square kilometres of the Magaliesberg offering towering quartzite peaks and grassy vleis that allow visitors incredible views and some impressive hikes.

Gold-Reef-City

Gold Reef City is the Premier family entertainment complex offering a wide variety of thrilling entertainment for the entire family!
